HOME PAGE — ntsama.github.io Jean Marie Ntsama Philosopher of Language • AI & Cognition Researcher • Founder of the Pedagogical Trapeze Model Welcome I am a transdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of language, thought, learning, and artificial intelligence. My work bridges philosophy, linguistics, cognitive science, educational technology, and AI ethics, with a unique Africa–Europe perspective. I am the author of the Pedagogical Trapeze & Swing Model, a dynamic framework that redefines the learner–teacher–knowledge–technology relationship in the age of AI. My research explores: how language and thought co‑develop how learning is transformed by digital tools and AI how neurodiversity (dyslexia, aphasia, autism) reshapes pedagogy how African contexts enrich global theories of cognition and education Research Focus Language–Thought Co‑Development AI‑Augmented Learning Neurodiversity & Inclusion Digital Literacies in African Contexts Computational Modeling of Learning Featured Work The Pedagogical Trapeze & Swing Model A four‑actor dynamic system (Teacher–Learner–Knowledge–Technology) driven by motivation, curiosity, and interaction, anchored on an evolving point of support.
