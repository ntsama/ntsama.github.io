Jean Marie Ntsama

Philosopher of Language • AI & Cognition Researcher • Founder of the Pedagogical Trapeze Model

Welcome

I am a transdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of language, thought, learning, and artificial intelligence. My work bridges philosophy, linguistics, cognitive science, educational technology, and AI ethics, with a unique Africa–Europe perspective.

I am the author of the Pedagogical Trapeze & Swing Model, a dynamic framework that redefines the learner–teacher–knowledge–technology relationship in the age of AI.

Research Focus

  • Language–Thought Co‑Development
  • AI‑Augmented Learning
  • Neurodiversity & Inclusion
  • Digital Literacies in African Contexts
  • Computational Modeling of Learning
