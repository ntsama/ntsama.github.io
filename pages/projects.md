# 🇬🇧 English Version

## 🔬 Current Research Projects

### **1. Cognitive Trapeze Model — Theoretical & Computational Development**  
A dynamic framework modeling the interaction between **language**, **thought**, and **learning**.  
Current work includes:  
- formalizing the model computationally  
- integrating AI‑mediated learning environments  
- exploring neurodiversity and multilingual cognition  

### **2. AI‑Augmented Learning Environments**  
Designing and analyzing learning systems where AI supports:  
- conceptual development  
- language acquisition  
- writing and digital literacies  
- inclusive pedagogy  

### **3. Language–Thought Dynamics in Neurodiverse Learners**  
Empirical and theoretical research on:  
- dyslexia  
- language disorders  
- cognitive compensation strategies  
- multimodal learning  

---

## 🌍 International & Cross‑Cultural Projects

### **4. Europe–Africa Research Axis**  
Developing collaborative research on:  
- multilingual education  
- digital literacies  
- inclusive pedagogy  
- cognitive modeling across cultural contexts  

### **5. Digital Literacies & Social Web**  
Studying how digital tools reshape:  
- writing practices  
- learning strategies  
- cognitive processes  
- didactic approaches  

---

## 🚀 Future Projects

- Computational simulation of the Cognitive Trapeze  
- AI‑based diagnostic tools for language disorders  
- Multilingual cognitive modeling  
- Cross‑continental research
