## 🎤 Academic Talks & Presentations

### **1. Language–Thought Dynamics: Foundations of the Cognitive Trapeze Model**  
Invited talk presenting the theoretical foundations of the Cognitive Trapeze Model, focusing on:  
- the interrelation between language and thought  
- cognitive development  
- implications for AI‑augmented learning

Conference presentation on:  
- the impact of digital tools on writing and cognition  
- multimodal learning  
- inclusive pedagogy in digital environments  

### **3. Neurodiversity, Dyslexia & Cognitive Compensation**  
Talk exploring:  
- language disorders  
- cognitive strategies  
- multimodal and adaptive learning
