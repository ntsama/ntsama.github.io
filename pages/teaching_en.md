# Teaching  
## Philosophy of Teaching  

My teaching is grounded in the idea that **teachers and learners are equal before knowledge and the tools of knowledge**.  
Learning is a dynamic movement between stability (concepts, schemas) and exploration (hypotheses, interpretations), aligned with my **Cognitive Trapeze & Pedagogical Swing Model**.

I design learning environments where students develop:  
- conceptual clarity  
- metacognitive awareness  
- critical reasoning  
- digital and AI literacies  
- multilingual and intercultural competence

# 2. Teaching in Priority Education Zones (ZEP)  
I coordinate pedagogical activities in **priority education zones**, focusing on:

- inclusive learning environments  
- AI‑supported scaffolding  
- digital empowerment  
- multilingual practices

# 3. Pedagogical Innovations  

### AI‑Augmented Pedagogy  
I integrate AI tools to support:  
- adaptive feedback  
- multimodal reasoning  
- conceptual modeling  
- self‑regulated learning  

### Cognitive Scaffolding  
I design activities that help learners:  
- build stable conceptual anchors  
- explore new hypotheses  
- articulate reasoning processes  

### Multilingual & Intercultural Learning  
I promote the use of multiple languages as cognitive resources.
