# IA_4_ZEP  
# Artificial Intelligence for Education in Priority Zones  
# Intelligence Artificielle pour l’Éducation en Zone d’Éducation Prioritaire

---

# 🇬🇧 English Version

## 🌍 Vision & Mission  
> “Integrate AI tools into learning for 50 schools by 2027.”  
Empowering African teachers and learners through digital intelligence.

The IA_4_ZEP project aims to transform learning conditions in priority education zones by providing AI‑powered tools, adaptive resources, and teacher training programs.

---

## 📘 Context  
- Cameroon’s 2025–2026 policy: *“Security, Health & Learning in the AI Age”*  
- 70% of secondary schools in Ngaoundéré lack digital tools  
- 15,000 students and 1,200 teachers potentially impacted  
- Fully aligned with SDG 4 (Quality Education)

---

## 🧭 SWOT Analysis  
**Strengths** — Government support, local expertise  
**Weaknesses** — Limited connectivity, initial skills gap  
**Opportunities** — MINESSEC, UNESCO, Google for Education  
**Threats** — Cultural resistance, network instability

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
**General Objective:**  
Integrate AI in 50 schools across ZEP Ngaoundéré.

**Specific Objectives:**  
- Train 500 teachers & learners  
- Produce 100 pedagogical videos (FR & Fulfulde)  
- Achieve 80% adoption rate  

---

## 🛠 Key Outputs  
- Adaptive learning tools  
- Chatbots  
- Video generators  
- Auto‑translation  
- Mind maps  
- Example: 5‑minute AI‑generated video of *Le Petit Prince* in French & Fulfulde

---

## 📅 Implementation Plan (12 months)  
1. **Preparation (M1–2)** — Recruitment, needs assessment  
2. **Development (M3–6)** — Platform + 100 videos  
3. **Deployment (M7–9)** — Training in 20 schools  
4. **Sustainability (M10–12)** — Monitoring, handover  

---

## 🏛 Governance  
- **Project Director:** Jean Marie Ntsama  
- **Tech Team:** 3 AI developers  
- **Pedagogical Team:** 4 teachers  
- **Steering Committee:** Regional Delegate, Inspectors, Student Representative  
- Weekly meetings via Google Workspace  

---

## 🤝 Partnerships  
- **Institutional:** MINESSEC Adamaoua, University of Ngaoundéré  
- **Technological:** Orange Cameroun, Perplexity AI, Grok  
- **International:** UNESCO (SDG 4), Google for Education  
- **Local:** Teachers’ associations of Ngaoundéré  

---

## 💰 Budget Breakdown  
**Total:** 45,000,000 FCFA (75,000 USD)

- Team salaries — 15M  
- Training — 5M  
- Marketing — 2M  
- AI subscriptions — 8M  
- Logistics — 15M  

---

## 📊 Impact (2027)  
- 15,000 learners  
- 1,200 teachers  
- 50 connected pilot schools  
- 100 videos (FR & Fulfulde)  
- 80% satisfaction rate  

---

## 🔄 Sustainability  
- Teacher‑producer autonomy  
- Regional ownership  
- Open‑source AI platform  
- Continuous training

# 🇫🇷 Version Française

## 🌍 Vision & Mission  
> « Intégrer les outils d’IA dans l’apprentissage de 50 écoles d’ici 2027. »  
Autonomiser les enseignants et apprenants africains grâce à l’intelligence numérique.

---

## 📘 Contexte  
- Politique camerounaise 2025–2026 : *« Sécurité, Santé & Apprentissage à l’Ère de l’IA »*  
- 70 % des établissements secondaires de Ngaoundéré sans outils numériques  
- 15 000 apprenants + 1 200 enseignants concernés  
- Aligné sur l’ODD 4  

---

## 🧭 Analyse SWOT  
**Forces** — Soutien gouvernemental, expertise locale  
**Faiblesses** — Connectivité limitée, déficit initial de compétences  
**Opportunités** — MINESSEC, UNESCO, Google for Education  
**Menaces** — Résistance culturelle, instabilité réseau  

---

## 🎯 Objectifs  
**Objectif général :**  
Intégrer l’IA dans 50 écoles de la ZEP de Ngaoundéré.

**Objectifs spécifiques :**  
- Former 500 enseignants & apprenants  
- Produire 100 vidéos pédagogiques  
- Atteindre 80 % d’adoption  

---

## 🛠 Produits Clés  
- Outils d’apprentissage adaptatif  
- Chatbots  
- Générateurs vidéo  
- Traduction automatique
